# Performance Playbook

Use this reference when the task involves campaign structure, platform fit, keyword strategy, targeting, creative testing, or performance optimization.

## Platform Fit

### Google Ads

Best for:

- High-intent search demand
- Bottom-of-funnel acquisition
- Keyword-driven traffic

Focus on:

- Intent
- Keyword quality
- Negative keywords
- Ad-to-landing alignment

### Meta Ads

Best for:

- Scalable audience testing
- Retargeting
- Creative iteration
- Lead generation and mid-funnel conversion work

Focus on:

- Audience segmentation
- Creative testing
- Offer clarity
- Pixel and conversion setup

### LinkedIn Ads

Best for:

- B2B audiences
- Role- and company-based targeting
- Higher-value professional offers

### TikTok Ads

Best for:

- Attention-led creative
- Broad audience testing
- Early-to-mid funnel acquisition

### YouTube Ads

Best for:

- Video storytelling
- Awareness and consideration
- Retargeting support

## Google Search Structure

Use:

- Campaign
- Ad groups
- Keywords
- Ads

Keyword control:

- Exact match
- Phrase match
- Broad match carefully
- Negative keywords

## Keyword Strategy

Map:

- Primary keywords
- Secondary keywords
- Long-tail keywords

Check:

- Search volume
- Competition
- Cost per click
- Search intent

## Social Campaign Objectives

Common objectives:

- Traffic
- Leads
- Conversions
- Engagement
- Brand awareness

Choose the objective that matches the real business goal, not the easiest metric to improve.

## Targeting Strategy

Use:

- Interest targeting
- Behavior targeting
- Lookalike audiences
- Retargeting

## Retargeting

Good retargeting sources:

- Website visitors
- Cart abandoners
- Previous customers

## Budget Management

Protect budget by:

- Testing before scaling
- Scaling only working campaigns
- Pausing low performers quickly
- Separating test budgets from scale budgets

## Metrics To Watch

Core metrics:

- CTR
- CPC
- CPA
- ROAS
- Conversion Rate

Interpret them together, not in isolation.

Examples:

- High CTR with weak conversion can signal a landing-page or offer problem
- Low CTR can signal weak creative, bad audience fit, or poor keyword relevance
- High CPC can signal competition, weak quality, or poor targeting efficiency
- High CPA can signal funnel friction, bad targeting, or weak conversion quality

## Landing-Page Alignment

Check:

- Whether the page matches the promise of the ad
- Load speed
- Offer clarity
- CTA clarity
- Friction in forms or next steps

## A/B Testing

Always consider testing:

- Creatives
- Headlines
- Audiences
- Landing pages

## Tracking

Use:

- Google Analytics
- Meta Pixel
- Conversion tracking

Tracking should make it possible to connect spend to outcomes, not just clicks.

## Funnel Thinking

Map campaigns by stage:

- Awareness
- Interest
- Consideration
- Conversion

Different stages require different creatives, audiences, and KPIs.
